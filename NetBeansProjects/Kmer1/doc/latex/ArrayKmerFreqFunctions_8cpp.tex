{\footnotesize\ttfamily void Normalize\+Array\+Kmer\+Freq (\begin{DoxyParamCaption}\item[{\mbox{\hyperlink{classKmerFreq}{Kmer\+Freq}}}]{array\mbox{[}$\,$\mbox{]},  }\item[{int \&}]{n\+Elements,  }\item[{std\+::string}]{valid\+Nucleotides }\end{DoxyParamCaption})}



Normalizes the given array of \mbox{\hyperlink{classKmerFreq}{Kmer\+Freq}}. That is, for each \mbox{\hyperlink{classKmer}{Kmer}} in the array, all its characters are converted to uppercase. Then, invalid characters are replaced by the UNKNOWN\+\_\+\+NUCLEOTIDE value. 

If after the previous normalization process of every kmer, identical kmers are obtained, these will be merged into the first identical kmer by adding their frequencies. For example, suposse the following list of kmers\+: 4 Ct 5 hG 4 nG 1 cT 4

After the process of normalization of every kmer, we obtain the following list of kmers\+:

4 CT 5 \+\_\+G 4 \+\_\+G 1 CT 4

The final step will transform the list into\+: 2 CT 9 \+\_\+G 5
